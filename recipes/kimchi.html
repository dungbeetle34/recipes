Kimchi (50 hours)

Description

Kimchi is such a simple healthy food and you won't regret learning to make this delicious side dish

Ingredients

  • Napa Cabbage
  • Non-Iodized Salt
  • Korean Pepper Flakes
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Sugar
  • Carrot or Daikon Radish
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. Sterilize everything
  2. Brine the cabbage
  3. Flavor it up
  4. Mix
  5. Ferment
